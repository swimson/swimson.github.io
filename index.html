Hiking Trails Project
Summer 2020


I am researching on how people choose an outdoor activity. Feedback from volunteers like you will be incorporated into the design of a hiking trail search engine.


Participating in this project will involve 5 steps....

  1. Planning a trip
  2. Navigating to your location
  3. Doing your activity
  4. Post to your social media (optional)
  5. Participating in a retrospective interview.

Still interested? Want to participate?
Read on....


Important Information

  • Your participation in this project is voluntary
  • Project feedback will be incorporated into a research paper.
  • By participating, you consent to the use of your feedback in this analysis.
  • You can stop your participation or withdraw consent at any time.
  • There is no compensation for your participation.

Here's how you can join this project....


Step #1 - Planning

For this step, pick 3 possible places that you want to explore. Later you will pick only one activity to actually do. Right now, brainstorm some ideas. You could plan a trip to a hiking trail, a bike ride along a lake, an urban hike in the city to see street art, a walk to a local park with your dog, a rock climbing trip, or really anything you find interesting as long as it is a new experience for you and gets you out of the house. Note: It does not need to be a hike. Use your best judgement and don't do anything that would put yourself in danger.


Once you have picked your top 3 options, send them in an email to simpkevin@gmail.com.


After that, I will reply with a worksheet to help you record your thoughts while you are participating in your activity, and will schedule your retrospective interview.



Thank you, and I look forward to speaking with you!
